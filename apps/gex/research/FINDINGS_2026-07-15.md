## UNIFYING DIAGNOSIS
Not an entry problem (entries beat random, are directionally right). It's an INSTRUMENT-to-EDGE MISMATCH:
directionally-correct entries catch a fat-tailed move distribution — mostly tiny moves (bleed on ATM 0DTE)
+ a rare big move (the +140%/+201% live winners). ATM 0DTE buying is the most expensive way to express a
small directional edge; it bleeds on everything but the tail. Every null tonight reduces to this.

## OPTIONS TO "MAKE IT WORK" (operator decision)
1. MAGNITUDE FILTER: only take big-move setups (deep flush / trend day), skip the many +0.1% entries that
   can't pay. Fewer, bigger. (Requires a magnitude predictor — the hard part.)
2. CHANGE INSTRUMENT: express the +0.1% edge with underlying/futures/ITM/spreads that don't bleed theta.
   ATM 0DTE is the wrong vehicle for a small directional edge.
3. LIVE WITH THE TAIL: keep ATM 0DTE, accept it only pays via the rare big winner, size so the tail funds
   the bleed (what today's live fires did).
