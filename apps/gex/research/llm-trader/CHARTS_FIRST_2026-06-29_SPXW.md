  clean 0DTE edge: breakouts get capped below the magnet and slow grinds bleed theta,
  while shorts fight growing floors and dip-buying. Correctly, I stayed **flat through the
  entire midday/afternoon pin** and only nibbled two breakout attempts — both kept tiny
  and cut within minutes when they stalled (one -13%, one +8%).
- **What went right:** selectivity (3 trades, ~49 holds), fast cuts on failed theses,
  reading the +gamma pin correctly and not forcing chop, exit timing on T1 and T3.

  momentum was too weak; I should have demanded the confirmed impulse break (which is
  exactly what T3 gave me). Waiting for T3's setup and skipping T2 would have improved
  net to ~+46%.
- **Net verdict:** Charts-first + GEX-confirm worked. One A+ trade, one justified small
  win, one avoidable small loss, and correct patience through a locked-pin regime.
